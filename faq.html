Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click on the Login button on top right, then choose Sign Up. Enter your email and password to create an account.
Is THE MOON free to use?
Yes, THE MOON is completely free. We offer premium features coming soon.
How can I submit news?
Contact our editorial team via the contact form or email us at news@themoon.news
How do I report an issue?
Use the contact form or email support@themoon.news with details of the issue.
How to enable dark mode?
Click on the moon/sun icon in the header to toggle between light and dark mode.
