Content Writing Tips

📝 Headline Tips

Keep headlines under 70 characters. Use numbers, questions, and power words. Example: "10 Ways to..." instead of "Ways to..."

📊 SEO Optimization

Use keywords naturally. Write meta descriptions under 160 characters. Add internal links to related articles.

🖼️ Image Guidelines

Use high-quality images (1200x630px). Add alt text for accessibility. Compress images under 200KB for faster loading.

📱 Mobile First

Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences). Use subheadings every 2-3 paragraphs. Ensure CTAs are thumb-friendly.

⏱️ Reading Time

Aim for 3-5 minute reads (600-1000 words). Break long content with images, quotes, or bullet points.
