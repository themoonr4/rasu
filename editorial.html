Editorial Guidelines

Accuracy & Fact-Checking

All news must be verified from at least two reliable sources. Cross-check facts before publishing. Correct errors immediately with editor's note.

Originality

Content must be original. Give proper attribution when quoting sources. Avoid plagiarism at all costs.

Tone & Voice

Maintain neutral, professional tone. Avoid sensationalism. Present facts without bias. Use clear, simple language.

Conflicts of Interest

Disclose any potential conflicts. Avoid publishing content that promotes personal interests. Maintain editorial independence.
